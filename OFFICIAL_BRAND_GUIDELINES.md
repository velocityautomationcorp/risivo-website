## üí¨ Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Brand Voice Characteristics

#### Professional
Knowledgeable and trustworthy, speaking to business leaders with respect for their expertise.

#### Empowering
Confident and encouraging, helping users feel capable of achieving their growth goals.

#### Clear & Direct
Straightforward communication that gets to the point without unnecessary complexity.

### Writing Guidelines

#### DO:
‚úÖ Use active voice  
‚úÖ Write in second person (you, your)  
‚úÖ Focus on benefits and outcomes  
‚úÖ Use contractions for friendliness  
‚úÖ Be specific and actionable

#### DON'T:
‚ùå Use jargon without explanation  
‚ùå Sound overly casual or flippant  
‚ùå Make promises you can't keep  
‚ùå Use negative or limiting language  
‚ùå Be vague or ambiguous
